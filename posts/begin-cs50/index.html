Begin CS50

  • March 24, 2019
  • CS50  

The journey begins with CS50.

I’ve started this course a few times over the years, and only made it through the first two or three problem sets before my attention moved elsewhere. The experience I have had with it so far has been very positive. The content is very well produced and is refreshed often if I remember correctly. The instructor, David Malan, is very engaging and a pleasure to learn from. The community and energy around this class is outstanding - likely because CS50 is often a starting point for people in technology. I’m excited to start this course - one last time - and see it through to the end.

Even though I’ve started this course a few times in the past, I plan on starting from the beginning once again, and not re-using any of my previous work. I haven’t cracked open the syllabus just yet, so I’m not certain at the moment about what I can or should post on a public GitHub repository. I will likely only post assignments that are of my creation, and not any standard assignment answers. I will confirm this soon.

Here we go!
