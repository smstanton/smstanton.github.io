CS50 Week 1

  • April 16, 2019
  • CS50  

I finished Week 1 the week before last. It wasn’t much of a struggle as I’ve been through this week before. I finished the Hello, Mario (More), and Credit in pretty quick succession.

The Credit assignment was fun because it has some overlap in what I do in my day job. We work closely with payment gateways and credit card processing on some of our projects, so I ended up in familiar territory with this assignment.

I am most of the way through Week 2 at the moment. I completed Caesar and Vigenere earlier this week, and am part way through Crack. I will do a full post on Week 2 when I wrap it up.
