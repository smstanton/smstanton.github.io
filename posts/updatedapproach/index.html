Updated Approach

  • June 3, 2019
  • Meta  

Hot off the heels of a much needed vacation, and also of a big gap in my studies, its time to re-commit and update my approach to this endeavor. I’m going to focus on completing one chapter/lecture/problem set or other “unit” of work per day and to get either a twitter post or a blog post done each day as well. Posts to twitter will be “accountability posts”, which are done as a way of holding myself accountable by feeling some pressure to check-in daily. Posts to this blog are for when I have something more substantial to say.

Also, CS50 is going on the back burner for a bit while I divert attention to one of the course’s recommended texts, Programming in C (3rd Edition), by Stephen Kochan. I’ve been struggling with the Crack problem set, and I realize most of my struggle is in getting C to do what I need it to do.
